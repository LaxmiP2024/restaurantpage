Paradise Biryani

Paradise Biryani is a Hyderabad based firm which owns the iconic biryani and kebab brand Paradise. Existing institutional investor Samara Capital Group is investing in Paradise through a combination of primary infusion and secondary purchase of shares from founders. The primary infusion shall be used to grow the restaurant footprint from 50 to about 500 in the coming few years. Started in 1953, Paradise is the largest biryani focused player in the country and is one of the most well-known brands in the Indian food services space. Biryani category has seen significant investor interest over the last few years and has consistently been the most ordered item on food aggregators’ apps in each of the last 5 years.
